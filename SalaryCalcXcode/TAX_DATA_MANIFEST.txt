   - Official country name

   - Tax year definition

2. Income Tax System
   - Progressive tax brackets (if applicable)
   - Flat rates (if applicable)
   - Personal allowances/basic exemptions
   - Standard deductions
   - Tax-free thresholds

   - Social security rates (employee & employer)
