## 📄 App Description (4000 characters max)

### Primary Description:
SalaryCalc is the world's first intelligent salary calculator that instantly converts gross salary to net take-home pay across 35+ countries and regions.

Whether you're relocating internationally, evaluating job offers, or planning your finances, SalaryCalc provides accurate, up-to-date tax calculations in seconds.

**✨ Key Features:**

📊 **Instant Salary Calculations**
- Calculate net salary in 35+ countries and regions
- Support for 50 US states and 10 Canadian provinces
- Accurate progressive tax bracket calculations
- Real-time effective tax rate display

🌍 **Global Coverage**
- Americas: USA, Canada, Mexico, Brazil, Argentina, Chile
- Europe: UK, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, Switzerland, Sweden, Denmark, Norway, Poland
- Asia-Pacific: India, China, Japan, Singapore, Australia, Hong Kong, South Korea
- Middle East: UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar
- Complete with federal, state/provincial, and local taxes

🗣️ **8 Languages**
- English, Spanish, French, German
- Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, Portuguese
- Supports any language preference

💰 **Detailed Tax Breakdown**
- Federal/National income tax
- State/Provincial/Regional taxes
- Social security contributions
- Medicare and health insurance
- Pension and retirement contributions
- All major deductions itemized

🔄 **Salary Comparison Tool**
- Compare salaries across multiple countries
- See net salary differences instantly
- View effective tax rates by region
- Plan your international relocation
- Ranked results by net take-home pay

⚡ **Fast & Accurate**
- Calculations complete in <50ms
- Uses official 2024-2025 tax rates
- Verified against government sources
- No internet required (offline-first)

🔒 **Privacy First**
- All calculations happen locally
- No salary data is collected
- No tracking or analytics
- No accounts required
- Your data stays on your device

🎨 **Beautiful Design**
- Clean, intuitive interface
- Dark mode support
- iPad optimized
- Accessibility features
- VoiceOver compatible
