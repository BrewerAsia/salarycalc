Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2025

Overview

SalaryPro ("we", "our", "the app") is a salary calculator application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your salary information
  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal details
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not transmit your calculation data to any server

All salary calculations are performed entirely on your device. Your financial data never leaves your phone.

Local Storage

Your calculation history and app preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time from within the app or by deleting the app.

Advertising

SalaryPro displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve relevant ads:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • App usage data

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

SalaryPro requests your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites, in compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You may decline this request and still use all app features.

Children's Privacy

SalaryPro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
