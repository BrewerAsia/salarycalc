## 1. Introduction

CalcPro ("App," "we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of information in our iOS calculator application.

**Key Principle:** CalcPro is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT track your usage
- We do NOT require user accounts or login
- We do NOT collect device identifiers
- We do NOT use analytics or behavioral tracking
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT share data with third parties

### 2.2 Information Stored Locally
All information is stored exclusively on your device:
- **Calculation History:** Stored locally using iOS UserDefaults
- **User Preferences:** Display settings stored locally (decimal places, currency symbol, theme)
- **No Cloud Sync (Phase 1):** Data is never sent to our servers

### 2.3 No Third-Party Services
CalcPro does not integrate with or share data to:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Cloud services
- Any external servers

## 3. How We Use Information

Since we collect no personal data, there is no usage of personal information.

Locally-stored calculation history and preferences are used solely to:
- Display your calculation history
- Restore your preferences when you reopen the app
- Improve local app performance

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Local Storage Protection
- All data is protected by iOS file system encryption
- Data exists only on your device
- No data leaves your device (unless you explicitly share it)

### 4.2 No Data Breaches Risk
Since we don't collect or transmit data:
- Your calculations cannot be intercepted
- No central database means no server-side breach risk
- Your financial calculations remain private to your device

### 4.3 App Updates & Data
- You control all local data
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data
- Reinstalling the app provides a clean slate
- Backups to iCloud are controlled by your device settings

## 5. Children's Privacy

CalcPro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for all ages and contains no content specifically targeting children.

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Data Access
- All your data is stored on your device and directly accessible to you
- You can view calculation history within the app

### 6.2 Data Deletion
- Clear app data: Settings > General > iPhone Storage > CalcPro > Offload App
- Delete app: Long press app icon > Remove App > Delete App
- All local data is permanently deleted

### 6.3 Data Portability
- Export calculations via share feature (coming Phase 2)
- Copy results to clipboard
- No vendor lock-in
